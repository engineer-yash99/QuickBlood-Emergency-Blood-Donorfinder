Frequently Asked Questions

Most donors report feeling only a brief pinch when the needle is inserted. The actual donation is painless, and many donors read, use their phones, or chat with staff during the process.
Your body replaces the donated blood volume within 24-48 hours. Red blood cells are fully replenished in 4-6 weeks. Most people feel completely normal within a few hours.
Eat a healthy meal, drink plenty of water, get a good night's sleep, and bring a valid ID. Avoid alcohol for 24 hours before donation and don't donate on an empty stomach.
